Projects

Replica of the official website of Dove (May 2018)

Inspired by the website of Dove, I made a replica. Some highlights of what’s working:

  • Multilevel menu in header. If you click on a link, a full-screen menu appears, with expandable sections.
  • Shopping bag in header. When you hover over the bag icon, a block with the contents of your shopping bag drops out.
  • Expandable marketing copy on the page.
  • Language switcher buttons in the footer. (They don’t actually switch language, but clicking one of them will make it active.)
  • Responsive design. Everything is mobile-ready. On mobile phones and tablets, all navigation links fold into the hamburger icon.

Tools I used to build it: HTML, CSS (SCSS), JavaScript (jQuery), Gulp, Git, Netlify.
